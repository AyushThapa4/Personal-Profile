About Me

My name is Ayush Thapa, and I am a student at Daffodil School, currently studying in grade 9. I come from a loving and supportive family. My father, Dinesh Thapa, works abroad, dedicating his efforts to provide for our family and ensure that we have everything we need. His commitment and hard work have always been a source of inspiration for me, and I admire him for the sacrifices he makes to give us a better life.

On the other hand, my mother, Mamatha Thapa, is the heart of our home. She is a housewife, and while some may underestimate the value of this role, I know that what she does is invaluable. She manages our household with grace and ensures that everything runs smoothly. From cooking delicious meals to taking care of all the little details that make our home a warm and welcoming place, my mother is truly the backbone of our family.


As a student at Daffodil School, I take my studies seriously and strive to do my best in all my subjects. Being in grade 9, I understand the importance of this academic year as it lays the foundation for my future studies. My school life is filled with learning, exploring new ideas, and developing skills that will help me in my future endeavors.


In addition to my academic pursuits, I also have a deep interest in extracurricular activities. Whether it's participating in school events, engaging in sports, or exploring creative arts, I believe in a well-rounded education that goes beyond the classroom. My teachers and friends at Daffodil School have always encouraged me to pursue my passions and excel in everything I do.


Growing up in a family where both my parents play such significant roles in shaping my life, I have learned the importance of hard work, dedication, and love. My father’s resilience and my mother’s nurturing nature have taught me valuable lessons that I carry with me every day. Their support motivates me to aim high and work diligently towards achieving my goals.


Looking ahead, I aspire to continue my education with the same enthusiasm and determination. I want to make my parents proud by not only excelling academically but also by being a person of strong character and integrity. I know that with the foundation my parents have provided and the education I am receiving at Daffodil School, I am well on my way to building a bright and successful future.
